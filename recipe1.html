The Best Banana Bread Recipe

There’s no such thing as over-ripe bananas when it comes to banana bread!

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 stick (8 tbsp) of unsalted, room temperature butter
  • 2 eggs at room temperature
  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • 1.5 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 6 ounces of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  1. Preheat the oven to 325 Fahrenheit. Spray a 9x5x3 in. glass loaf pan with butter and line it with parchment paper
  2. Using an electric mixer (or by hand), cream the sugar and butter until it becomes a homogenous mixture. Beat the eggs into the sugar and butter mixture one at a time.
  3. In a bowl, mash the bananas with a fork. It is ok if there are some small chunks of banana. Mix in the cinnamon and milk
  4. In another bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Add the banana mixture to the creamed butter mixture. Make sure it is well combined.
  6. Slowly pour the dry ingredients (flour mixture) into the banana/butter mixture. Mix until the white flour isn’t visible in the batter.
  7. Fold the chocolate chips into the batter until evenly dispersed.
  8. Pour the batter into the parchment-paper-lined pan. Bake for 70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the bread comes out without wet batter.
  9. Allow the fully baked loaf to cool for 20 minutes before removing it from the pan.
  10. Slice the loaf and enjoy it with a glass of milk!
